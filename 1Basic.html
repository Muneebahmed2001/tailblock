Saylani Welfare International is a non-profit organization based in Pakistan that focuses on various social welfare initiatives. It was founded by Maulana Bashir Ahmed Farooqui in 1999. The organization is involved in providing humanitarian aid, education, healthcare, and vocational training to the underprivileged. Saylani Welfare is known for its extensive charity work, including food distribution, orphan care, and skill development programs.
